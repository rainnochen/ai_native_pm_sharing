# Quality Contract

Purpose: define what good AI-assisted PM work must satisfy.

## Contract

| Dimension | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Evidence | Claims are tied to sources or marked as assumptions |
| Decision | Output supports a specific decision |
| Traceability | Inputs, assumptions, and changes are recorded |
| Safety | Sensitive data and high-risk actions are gated |
| Testability | Recommendations include validation steps |
| Usability | Templates are copyable by PMs |

## Review Questions

1. What decision does this output support?
2. What evidence is strongest?
3. What assumptions remain untested?
4. What could go wrong if we follow this?
5. What human decision is required?
