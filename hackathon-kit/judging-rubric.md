# Judging Rubric

Purpose: score a hackathon project from the judge's point of view.

| Dimension | 1 | 3 | 5 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Problem | vague | clear but narrow evidence | urgent, specific, evidence-backed |
| Demo | mostly slides | partial working path | reliable live demo with fallback |
| AI Native | AI decorative | AI useful in one step | AI essential to workflow |
| Technical Feasibility | hand-wavy | plausible prototype | clear architecture and constraints |
| Impact | unclear | moderate user value | strong user/business potential |
| Risk Handling | ignored | known risks listed | risks tested or mitigated |
| Storytelling | confusing | understandable | memorable and decision-oriented |

## Output

```markdown
## Score Summary
## Strongest Evidence
## Biggest Risk
## Recommended Fix Before Submission
```

## Quality Checks

- Do not over-score polish.
- Reward evidence and demo reliability.
- Penalize hidden manual steps.
