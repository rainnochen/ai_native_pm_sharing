# Code of Conduct

This project is for practical, respectful collaboration around AI-native product management.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Explain tradeoffs clearly.
- Keep examples anonymized and safe.
- Challenge ideas without attacking people.
- Respect license, attribution, and privacy boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Sharing secrets, private data, or internal confidential information.
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Misrepresenting sources or benchmarks.
- Copying restricted-license content without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close issues, or block contributors who violate these rules.
